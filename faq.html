Frequently Asked Question

Here is the list of questions and answers for the SEAMEO RECFON Internship Program. If your question is not included in the list below, you can send your question via email to
hr@seameo-recfon.org

How to apply for an internship at SEAMEO RECFON?

To apply for an internship, please visit the following link: internship.seameo-recfon.org and click the log in/register button. Please fill in your personal data completely and accurately, and complete the required documents and form to apply for the internship.

How can I find out the results of the internship application at SEAMEO RECFON?

You will be notified of the results of your internship application via email. Please check your inbox or spam folder for the notification.

When is the internship application period open at SEAMEO RECFON?

The internship application period opens every year from June to July. Please keep an eye on the official website for exact dates.

How long is the internship duration at SEAMEO RECFON?

The internship duration at SEAMEO RECFON typically lasts for 3 to 6 months, depending on the program and requirements.
