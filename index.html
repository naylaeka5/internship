Frequently Asked Question

How to apply for an internship at SEAMEO RECFON?

To apply for an internship, please visit the following link: internship.seameo-recfon.org and click the log in/register button, Please fill in your personal data completely and accurately, Complete the required documents and form in order to apply the internship.

How can I find out the results of the internship application at SEAMEO RECFON?

The internship application results will be announced on the applicant dashboard on the internship.seameo-recfon.org website. Please also kindly check your email regularly for any updates.

Can I apply for multiple positions at SEAMEO RECFON?

You can only apply for one position for this internship.

Is there a selection process to participate in this internship program?

There is a required documents screening selection process conducted by the HR team of SEAMEO RECFON.

What if I forget my password for my account?

If you forget your password when logging in, kindly contact our admin at hr@seameo-recfon.org using the same email address you used for your application. We will assist you in resetting your password.

How can I correct the application data that I inputted (mistyped/typo)?

You can correct any documents or form from the applicant dashboard. However, if you need to correct your profile information, please contact the admin at hr@seameo-recfon.org.
